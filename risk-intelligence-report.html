Coverage Gaps From Recent Bank Audits

The D&O Policy That Won't Protect Your Directors

Gap: Every community bank D&O policy I have reviewed contains some form of exclusion for claims "arising out of" a cyber incident. That means, if a data breach triggers an FDIC enforcement action or a lawsuit, the board has zero D&O coverage.

Impact: The policy your directors believe protects them will not respond.

The Vendor Outage Nobody Sized

Gap: Of the five community banks I have reviewed, dependent business interruption sublimits ranged from zero (coverage not purchased) to $1M. Every one of these banks runs on a single core processor. A multi-day outage would exceed the sublimit before recovery begins.

Impact: Potentially seven figures of uninsured exposure per incident. The board had never tested the limit against actual vendor dependence.
