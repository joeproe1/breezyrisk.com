What I Have Found in Bank Audits

A community bank's wire fraud coverage on the fidelity bond had a 50% co-payment clause. The broker never flagged this. Half of the loss would have been unrecoverable.

Three policies, none of them paid. After a data breach, the cyber policy excluded board defense. The D&O had a cyber carve-out. The fidelity bond covered employee dishonesty, but not external attacks. Directors were on their own.

A $2M cyber policy covered business interruption, but sublimited vendor outages to $100K. If the bank's core processor went down, coverage capped at 5% of the limit.

I have reviewed policies for five community banks and credit unions. Every one had coverage gaps the broker missed.
