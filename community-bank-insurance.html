Gaps I Keep Finding

Examiners check your controls. They check whether you have insurance. They don't check whether your insurance covers the gaps. In the financial institutions I have reviewed, these are the gaps that show up every time.

Board members personally exposed

After a breach, regulators investigate the board. The cyber policy says ‘governance matter.’ The D&O says ‘cyber event.’ Both deny. Directors are on their own.

Wire fraud: only half covered

A fraudulent wire hits. The bond only covers half because of a 50% co-payment clause nobody flagged.

Coverage that was never presented

Social engineering protection on the bond. Vendor outage coverage on the cyber policy. Available from your carrier, never offered to your bank.
